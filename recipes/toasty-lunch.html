Toasty Lunch

Description

Ingredients

  • Pita bread
  • Mozzarella
  • Ripe tomatoes
  • Red or green pesto
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt 'n pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the pita in two
  2. Put the two pita parts of the pita in the toaster for a minute
  3. Slice the tomatoes
  4. Sprinkle some olive oil on the inside of the pita parts
  5. Put some salt 'n pepper on in
  6. Put some pesto on the pita parts
  7. Slice up the mozzarella and put it on one of the pita parts
  8. Put some sliced tomatoe parts on top of it
  9. Put the other half of the pita on top
  10. Toast until golden brown
