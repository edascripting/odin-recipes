Toasty Lunch

Description

Ingredients

  • Yoghurt
  • Banana
  • Strawberries
  • Granola
  • Chocolat Sprinkles

Steps

  1. Peel banana and cut in parts
  2. Put the banana parts and some strawberries in the blender
  3. Add some cups of yoghurt into the blender
  4. Mix it all up until it's smooth as silk
  5. Pour the contents of the blender into a breakfast bowl
  6. Add a few spoons of granola and stir lightly
  7. Finish it off with some chocolate sprinkles
  8. Enjoy your trusty old blender breakfast
